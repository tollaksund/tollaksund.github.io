Line-Following Robot

This robot was designed to follow black electrical tape laid out on a white surface using infrared reflectance sensors. It uses feedback control logic (PID) to dynamically adjust motor speeds and stay on track even around tight corners.

The robot was built using a custom 3D-printed chassis, DC motors with encoders, and a microcontroller programmed in C++. It was capable of consistent lap times and real-time error correction based on light intensity data.
