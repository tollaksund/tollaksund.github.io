Autonomous Warehouse Robot

This robot was designed as part of a mechatronics project to autonomously navigate a warehouse environment. It uses multiple sensors, including ultrasonic and infrared, along with a motorized drivetrain and microcontroller logic to detect obstacles and follow paths.

The goal was to reduce manual labor in warehouse management and demonstrate real-time obstacle avoidance and routing. The robot was 3D-printed, custom-wired, and coded in C++/Python depending on the microcontroller used.
