About Me

I’m Tollak Sund, a final year Mechanical Engineering student at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU). I recently completed an exchange semester abroad at Queensland University of Technology (QUT) in Australia, which further broadened my perspective on mechatronics and engineering design. My academic focus blends core mechanical systems, mechatronics, and product development, with hands-on experience in designing, programming, and building complex machines.

This website serves as a showcase of the projects I’ve built, demonstrating practical application of PID control, embedded C++ programming, and custom 3D design. I thrive on technical challenges that require integrating hardware and software solutions.
